\section*{Abstract}
The aim of this report is to characterize a diode-laser. Firstly, the output power of the laser diode is recorded for different inpdecreasesut-currents and three different temperatures, allows us to determine both threshold current and efficiency. We observe the threshold current going up with temperature, whilst the efficiency remains constant. The second part of the experiment includes a  Fabry-Perot interferometer, allowing us to study the frequency tuneability of the laser with respect to current and temperature. Overall, frequency decreases with both increasing temperature and current, with current adjustments being the finer ones.
